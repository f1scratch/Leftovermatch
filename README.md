# Leftovermatch
ISU project

Have you ever wondered about what to cook for dinner? This is a common daily challenge, which I aim to solve with my app, LeftoverMatch. This app allows you to search with the ingredients you have (even if they're random), pick your dietary preference to suggest appropriate recipes, and enter your allergies to filter out any recipe that contains them. This recommendation engine makes your daily life easier by thinking for you and bringing you recipes that fit your needs. This app is highly user-friendly and works effectively, and is a reliable tool that can help various target audiences, such as parents who cook and students who will be living on their own, potentially in residence. I hope to improve this tool by populating my database further and making the recipe instructions more detailed.
